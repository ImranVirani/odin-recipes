Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a an italian dish that is popular for it's taste.

Lasagna consists of layers of meat and pasta

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodes
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodes.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
