Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar Salad is a dish that is healthy and tasty.

Caesar Salad has Caesar dressing in it.

Ingredients

  • 2 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped, or to taste
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup half-and-half
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons Worshestershire Sauce

Steps

  1. Combine anchovy fillets with garlic in food processor. Pulse several times to form a paste
  2. Process mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, half-and-half, lemon juice, Dijon mustand, and Worshestershire sauce with anchovy mixture until dressing is creamy
  3. Refridgerate for 1 hour
  4. Pour dressing in salad bowl with lettuce in it and toss
  5. serve with slices of parmesan cheese.
